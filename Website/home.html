ABOUT US

QUINGS COMESTICS is a brand producing skincare and makeup products out to encourage and promote skin confidence and pride, QUINGS stand for QUEENS and KINGS which symbolizes the authority of confidence when the skin is skinning. We are a Cameroonian-based brand and our products are purely organic and professionally synthesized in partnership with botanical gardens from Switzerland. Our vision is use our brand as a voice to impact the world by showing people the glory in embracing their skin heritage.
